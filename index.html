Our Story...

In 2020, Juliette R followed her heart to the UK, a journey fueled by love for coffee. There, she sowed the seeds of "Beanstalk Coffee." With every cup poured, she shared her passion, brewing a sense of belonging. The aroma entwined stories of distant lands, and the café buzzed with connections. Beanstalk Coffee grew beyond a café, becoming a beacon of community and warmth. Juliette's love for coffee intertwined with the town's heartbeat, a testament to how a single cup can hold friendship, dreams, and a sprinkle of magic.

Our Coffee...

Introducing our pride and joy: Coffea arabica, the cornerstone of Beanstalk Coffee's exquisite offerings. Grown in the finest coffee regions worldwide, these beans epitomize the artistry of flavor. With a sip of our Arabica brew, expect an enchanting journey through a symphony of taste notes. Delicate hints of jasmine and citrus intertwine with velvety undertones of dark chocolate and toasted almonds, creating an experience that dances upon the palate.

Each cup is a testament to the meticulous care put into cultivating and roasting these precious beans. Arabica's distinct smoothness and lower acidity deliver a brew that's both gentle and exhilarating. We celebrate the subtleties and complexities of these beans, showcasing their origin's unique terroir and character. At Beanstalk Coffee, our Coffea arabica embodies the soul of our endeavor – a cup of boundless passion and discovery for you to savor with every sip.
